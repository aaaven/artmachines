Zichi | 子期

January, 2019
Sam

“ZICHI” is an interactive musical AI. Being more than a playable instrument, “ZICHI” is also a musical AI who is able to understand the input from participants, and accordingly compose a new melody to playback. Guqin is an eastern traditional musical instrument with a long history and a beautiful legend: The music of the most talented musician and Guqin player Yu Boya can only be understood by Zichi. Their friendship and story were told over thousands of years to honor the value of understanding. With respect to Chinese traditional art form, the artist chooses Guqin music to train AI and name it with ZICHI aims to combine eastern traditional art experiences with emerging technology and AI so to present the old with new. This installation also seeks to establish a creative and collaborative AI profile and demonstrate how AI can enhance our creativity.


“子期”是一件交互音乐人工智能装置。除了是一把可供演奏的古琴，“子期”更是一个音乐AI，能理解参与者的演奏/输入并以之为灵感实时谱写新的乐章并返还给参与者。俞伯牙高山流水唯有钟子期能识能懂，古琴传承千载的除了东方的音乐传统还有这一段知音佳话。选择古琴这一东方意向创作AI，并以子期命名这个音乐AI，艺术家尝试连接东方艺术文化经验和当代的科技、人工智能技术。既是对传统文化的全新诠释，也致力寻找和建立一个积极的与人类协作并具备创造能力的AI形象。作为艺术家连续实践的议题-以人工智能赋能现代人、并传递传统文化精神的一部分，这件作品同时也是其装置计划“新文人计划”的一部分。



互动说明:
参与者随意拨弦或演奏古琴，乐音停止后静候，装置记录的参与者创作的乐曲将被输入AI系统，子期将依据
