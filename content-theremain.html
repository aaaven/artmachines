Therem{Ai}n | 爱琴

January, 2019
Sam

“Therem{ai}n” is an AI enhanced musical instrument that responds to, accompanies and inspires you. The user can play the musical instrument as a normal Theremin, and after the play is finished, the therem{ai}n will generate a new melody based on player's input and plays back in the tone and style of a Theremin. What this product focuses on is how AI can be used to work hand in hand with an activity that is inherently special to human beings, the creation of music. We are going beyond the novelty of an AI-based performance and homing in how in a world where AI is often seen as a replacement for jobs and production, that AI can accompany, assist, and respond to our creative process.


《爱琴》是一个AI赋能的乐器，不仅响应你，而且陪伴你，启发你。这个AI产品的重心在于思考人工智能在音乐谱写和演奏这一人类绝对主导的创造过程中的运用。不同于其他尝试替代人类或与人类竞争的人工智能运用，这个产品更在乎的是在当下人工智能被普遍视作洪水猛兽甚至将终结人类的大舆论语境下，人工智能如何在我们的生活和我们的创意和创造过程中起到响应，陪伴，以及辅助启发的作用。



互动说明:
用户挥动双手演奏特雷门琴，完毕后静候即可听到AI解读并谱写、演奏新的旋律。

*该产品由上海谷歌GDG孵化器孵化中（2019.01-ongoing）
